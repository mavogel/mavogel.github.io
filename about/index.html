About me

Hello, my name is Manuel Vogel. I’m striving to grow every day as a person and developer and I like doing good things and talk/write about them. (Kudos for the inspiration go to my buddy r3dsm0k3)

Currently I’m working at Stocard as a DevOps Engineer, doing awesome stuff with terraform, Docker, TypeScript and bash. In my free time I maintain the terraform-provider-docker.

I love automating things because I believe we’re all smart people and we should not be the robot but build the robot, for example by teaching non-engineering heroes how to achieve this well with this little black window, called terminal. So now they also love to automate small tasks of their daily life. Code should strive for simplicity and not complexity. Yes, we spent way more time debugging than actually writing the code. Tests can easily be written before, which is more fun though. Objects should be immutable, as well as the infrastructure.

Apart from the technical side, I am very proud to have the mental strength after two years to do yoga every day because it helps me to focus on my goals, strengthens my mindset and heals my body. This lead to the fact that close people around me are saying about me that I motivate them to focus on their goals, by asking the right questions and pushing them a little bit further into the direction they want to go. Constant reflection on my day, weeks and month help me to ensure that I stay on the right track towards my goals (which are SMART), but on the other hand, enjoy the path to the fullest as well.

This is my private blog and opinions are my own.

Thanks for reading!
